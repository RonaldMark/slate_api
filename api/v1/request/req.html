Requests

This API allows you to push a money transfer request to Switchlink.

There are various types of transactions:

  • ‘IPSL’ – Bank Transfer.

  • ‘B2C’ - Mobile Wallet Transfer.

  • ‘B2B’ - Business to Business Transfer.

  • ‘C2B’ - Customer to Business Transfer.

  • ‘CARD’ - ThreeDSecure (Also Known as “VerfifiedByVisa” OR “MasterCard Secure”).

There are various types of transactions:

  • ‘ENROL3DS’ – This confirms that the card is 3DS enrolled .

  • ‘VALIDATE3DS’ - This validates the authentication data from the iframe.
